## 2. Cloud Relay Subscription

VaultSync offers an optional Cloud Relay subscription for near-realtime push-based synchronization.

- **Price:** $0.99/month (USD)
- **Type:** Auto-renewable subscription, billed monthly through your Apple ID
- **Free usage:** The app is fully functional without a subscription. Without Cloud Relay, sync occurs when the app is opened and via background refresh.
- **Cancellation:** You can cancel at any time through your iOS Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.
- **Free trial:** If a free trial is offered, any unused portion is forfeited upon subscribing.

Payment is processed by Apple. VaultSync does not collect or store payment information.

## 3. Syncthing Protocol

VaultSync uses the Syncthing protocol for peer-to-peer file synchronization. Your data is synchronized directly between your devices. VaultSync does not store, access, or transmit your vault contents to any server controlled by VaultSync — except when using the optional Cloud Relay service for push notifications.
